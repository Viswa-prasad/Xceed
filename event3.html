Ace the K!ase



In the present world of Business, each day brings in a challenge. Your decision can make or break the future of the company you are associated with. "Ace the K!ase" event is the chance to showcase your managerial skill-sets and solve real-life problems in different settings such as decision-making, problem-solving, communication and innovation.

Type:
Team Event (1-3 persons in a team)

Pre-requisites :
A laptop/Pc with good internet connectivity

Round 1 : Quiz
This Round has questions based on aptitude, numerical ability, logical reasoning, general awareness. Participants should try and answer as many as they can in the given time duration of 45 minutes.

Rules :

  • It is a team event.
  • The participants can discuss with their team members through any means and submit single response for a team.
  • Total duration for round 1 is 45 minutes.

Round 2 : Ace the Case
It is basically a case study round where the students are given a case on which they have to solve and develop a PowerPoint presentation. Each team will have 10 mins to present it virtually.

Timing: 3 hrs

Rules :

  • Each team should make a ppt of atleast 7 slides.
  • Each participant, while presenting will compulsorily have to keep their camera on
  • All team members should be given importance.
  • Individual performance will be also taken into account.
