K! AWARDS

No person was ever honoured for what he received, honor has always been the reward for what he gave to society. - Calvin Coolidge The K! Awards revolve around the above principle and it is all about felicitating the unsung heroes of our nation, the people who designed their destiny against all odds. It focuses on the founding value of CTF which is to build socially responsible citizens. People ranging from budding innovators and educationalists to entrepreneurs and social activists are honoured for their selfless service which they have rendered to the society in their respective domains. K! Awards hosted by Kurukshetra'21 aims at mesmerizing its audience by bringing to their notice the extraordinary talents of our nation and by revering the unsung heroes for their service.
